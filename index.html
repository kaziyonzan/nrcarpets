Dyeing

Our artisans meticulously hand-dye yarns, using eco-friendly and AZO-free colors. This results in vibrant, long-lasting colors that reflect our commitment to environmentally friendly processes. Our technique provides both quality and sustainability, contributing to a more ecologically friendly textile sector.

Weaving & Knotting

The weaving process is quite wonderful, as our experienced artisans bring their creative visions to life. Each rug, whether flatweave, hand-tufted, or hand-knotted, is painstakingly handwoven with a careful eye for detail. Our handiwork epitomises accuracy and competence, ensuring the durability and exquisite texture of each rug.

Finishing Touches

Following the laborious weaving procedure, the rug receives a finishing touch. It all starts with painstaking pile trimming, which results in a smooth surface that highlights the design. Each knot and colour is then meticulously inspected, preserving the rug's unique tradition. Every step of the process, from trimming to washing to adding the crowning fringe finish, instills artistry in the rug, assuring everlasting beauty and enduring quality for future generations.
